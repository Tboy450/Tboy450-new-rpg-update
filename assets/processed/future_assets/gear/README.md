# Future Gear Assets

These transparent PNGs are unused future gear concepts.

- `drakefire_saber.png`: dragon/fire sword concept for a later fire or dragon quest.
- `sage_oath_staff.png`: healer-sage staff concept for a later Mage or support reward.
- `nightglass_daggers.png`: dark rogue dagger concept for a stealth or shadow reward.
- `lionheart_aegis.png`: lion-themed shield/charm concept for a defensive reward.
- `warrior_drakebreaker_axe.png`: Warrior heavy dragon/fire weapon concept.
- `warrior_guardian_plate.png`: Warrior armor concept.
- `warrior_lionheart_boots.png`: Warrior boots concept.
- `mage_star_lantern_staff.png`: Mage staff concept.
- `mage_ribbon_grimoire.png`: Mage spellbook/charm concept.
- `mage_firestorm_ring.png`: Mage fire accessory concept.
- `rogue_moonhook_blades.png`: Rogue weapon concept.
- `rogue_smokeveil_mask.png`: Rogue mask/armor concept.
- `rogue_nightstep_boots.png`: Rogue speed boots concept.
- `health_ribbon_vial.png`: shared healing item concept.
- `mana_star_phial.png`: shared mana item concept.
- `revival_lion_token.png`: shared revival/relic item concept.

To activate one later, first add a real equipment record in
`game_data/equipment.py`, then move or copy the PNG into
`assets/processed/equipment/`.

## Class Buckets

- Warrior concepts should lean heavy, defensive, lion, steel, and dragon-fire.
- Mage concepts should lean staff, spellbook, star, ribbon, firestorm, and sigil.
- Rogue concepts should lean speed, shadow, moon, smoke, dagger, and mask.
